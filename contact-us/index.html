Contact us

For technical issues with AVH tools, bug reports and questions about AVH data, please contact us at avh@chah.org.au.

For botanical questions, it is best to contact one of the participating herbaria. The Atlas of Living Australia (ALA) Natural History Collections site has their contact details.
